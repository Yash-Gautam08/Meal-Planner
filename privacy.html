Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

What data Mealique stores

Mealique stores your selected favorites, weekly plan, and shopping list in your browser local storage. This data stays on your device.

What data Mealique does not collect

Mealique does not require account signup, and it does not upload your meal plan to external servers in this version.

Third-party resources

The website may load fonts and recipe images from external CDNs. Those providers may log technical data such as IP address and request metadata.

Your control

You can remove saved planner data anytime by using the clear buttons in the planner or by clearing browser storage.

Contact

For support updates, use the project repository or contact page if added in future versions.
