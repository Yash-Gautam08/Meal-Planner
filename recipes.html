Banana Oats

A simple breakfast that keeps you full and energized.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup milk or plant milk

Steps

  1. Boil oats with milk.
  2. Add sliced banana.
  3. Cook for 2–3 minutes and serve warm.

Indian Lentil Dal

A classic plant-based protein source.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lentils
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • Tomato, garlic, spices

Steps

  1. Boil lentils until soft.
  2. Prepare spice tempering.
  3. Mix and simmer for 5 minutes.

Fresh Veg Salad

A light meal packed with vitamins.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Lemon & salt

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables.
  2. Mix with lemon and salt.
  3. Serve fresh.
