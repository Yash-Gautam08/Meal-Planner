Dietitians’ Guide

Evidence‑based notes on balanced plant‑based eating.

Key nutrients to watch

Protein, iron, calcium, B12, omega‑3s, iodine, zinc—plus tips to cover them.

  • Protein: legumes, tofu/tempeh, seitan, soy milk.
  • Iron: lentils, beans, seeds; pair with vitamin C.
  • Calcium: fortified milks, tofu, tahini, greens.

Common Beginner Mistakes

  • Too little protein
  • Skipping fruits
  • Over-reliance on junk vegan food

Sample Balanced Day

Breakfast: Oats + fruit

Lunch: Dal + rice + salad

Dinner: Tofu + veggies
